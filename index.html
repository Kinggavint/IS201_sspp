Pickleball Rules

  • Games are played to 11, win by 2.
  • Only the serving team can score points.
  • The ball must bounce once on each side after the serve.
  • No volleys in the non-volley zone ("the kitchen").

What You Need to Play

  1. Paddle
    • Composite or graphite preferred
    • Comfortable grip for control
  2. Pickleball (plastic ball with holes)
  3. Court shoes (non-marking)
